# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2024

## Introduction

Autovariant AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify app.

## Information We Collect

### Product Data
- Product names, descriptions, and metadata
- Product variants (sizes, colors, prices, stock levels)
- Product images and media files

### Store Information
- Shop domain and basic store information
- API access tokens (required for app functionality)

### Usage Data
- App usage statistics
- Error logs and performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Provide app functionality (creating and managing product variants)
- Match product images to variants using AI
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We use secure cloud infrastructure (Railway, Heroku, or similar)
- API tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- We do not store credit card or payment information

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to third parties. We only share data when:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to provide app functionality (e.g., Shopify API calls)
- You explicitly authorize us to do so

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Shopify APIs:** To manage products and variants
- **OpenAI API:** For AI-powered color matching (images are processed but not stored)
- **Hosting Provider:** To host the app (Railway, Heroku, etc.)

## Data Retention

- Product data is retained while your app is installed
- When you uninstall the app, we delete all associated data within 48 hours
- Some anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics purposes

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request data modification
- Request data deletion
- Opt out of data collection (by uninstalling the app)

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
